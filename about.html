About Sunrise Group of Industries

Excellence in mineral processing since decades. We are committed to delivering premium quality stone and mineral powders that power industries worldwide.

Our Story

Sunrise Group of Industries was founded with a vision to become the leading manufacturer of premium stone and mineral powders. What started as a small mineral processing unit has evolved into a comprehensive manufacturing enterprise serving diverse industries across the globe.

Our journey is built on the principles of quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Through decades of experience, we have perfected the art and science of transforming raw stones and minerals into high-grade powder products that meet the most stringent industrial requirements.

Today, we stand as a trusted partner for businesses worldwide, providing consistent quality products backed by technical expertise and reliable supply chain management.
