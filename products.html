Stone to Powder: Our Process

Understanding how we transform raw stones into premium powders

1. Raw Material Sourcing

Selection: We carefully select high-grade stones and minerals from trusted quarries and mines.

Quality Check: Each batch undergoes initial quality assessment for composition and purity.

Storage: Raw materials are stored in controlled environments to maintain quality.

2. Primary Processing

Crushing: Large stones are broken down using jaw crushers and impact crushers.

Screening: Materials are classified by size using vibrating screens.

Washing: Impurities are removed through washing and beneficiation processes.

3. Fine Processing

Grinding: Ball mills and roller mills achieve desired fineness levels.

Classification: Air classifiers separate particles by size distribution.

Purification: Magnetic separation removes unwanted minerals.

4. Quality Control & Packaging

Testing: Laboratory analysis confirms particle size, purity, and chemical composition.

Packaging: Products are packed in moisture-proof bags or bulk containers.

Storage: Final products stored in climate-controlled warehouses.
