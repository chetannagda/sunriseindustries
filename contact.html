Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services

What are your minimum order quantities?

Minimum orders vary by product, typically starting from 1 tonne. We're flexible with trial orders and can accommodate smaller quantities for testing purposes.

Do you provide custom specifications?

Yes, we specialize in custom powder solutions. Our technical team can develop products to meet your specific particle size, purity, and performance requirements.

What is your typical delivery time?

Standard products: 7-15 days from order confirmation. Custom products: 15-30 days depending on specifications. Express delivery available for urgent requirements.

Do you provide technical support?

Absolutely! Our experienced technical team provides application guidance, troubleshooting support, and optimization recommendations for your specific use cases.
